PRIVACY POLICY

What Personal Data I Collect And Why I Collect It

This website (Magnogen.net) protects any personal information a visitor might input into the site. It does not ever ask for any personal information. If the visitor were to type any personal information into a search bar or anything similar, the query does not get stored anywhere.

I request visitors to please check this page frequently to get updates. If a visitor should have any doubts or questions, please contact me on Github, or via Email.

1. Cookies

This website does not use cookies often, however where it does, all data saved in this form will be deleted when the visitor closes the browser session, as it is only stored on their device and not anywhere on the Internet.

2. Embedded Videos

Visitors can find my music on this site, and when they listen to it here, they watch an embedded video from YouTube.com. These embedded videos behave in exactly the same way as if the visitor were to go to the original site.

External websites may collect data about the visitor, use cookies, embed additional 3rd party tracking, and monitor the visitor's interaction with the embedded content, including recording the visitor's interaction with the embedded content if they have previously logged in on that website.

3. Advertisements

I a 3rd party advertising company (AdSense) to serve advertiements on apps I create. It uses the DART cookie to serve advertisements based on the visitor's interest and previous interactions. A visitor can always opt out of the DART cookie settings by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

  • If a visitor does not agree with this Privacy Policy, then please stop the usage of this site.
